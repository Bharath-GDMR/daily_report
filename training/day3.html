Day 3: Costing & Retail Terms

Objective: To master the financial and linguistic side of our business. Accurate costing and clear communication are vital for profitability and client satisfaction.

Morning Session: Zone-Wise Operations

Our projects are often managed by geographical zones. This session explains how this system works and why it's effective for large-scale campaigns.

  • Zonal Breakdown: Understand how we divide and manage projects across different regions.
  • Project Management Tools: An introduction to the software we use to track jobs, communicate with teams, and manage deadlines for each zone.
  • Case Studies: We will review past projects to illustrate how our zonal management system works in practice.

Mid-Morning Session: Purchase Orders (POs)

Purchase Orders are legally binding documents that form the backbone of our transactions. This session will clarify the entire PO workflow.

  • Client POs vs. Vendor POs: Understand the difference between the PO we receive from a client and the PO we issue to a vendor.
  • The Requisition Workflow: Follow the journey of a PO from initial client request to final payment, including internal approvals and documentation.
  • Reading the Fine Print: Learn to identify and understand key terms and conditions on a PO.

Afternoon Session: The Language of Retail & Print

Speak like a pro. This session is a deep dive into the specific terminology used in the retail and printing industries.

  • Glossary of Terms: We will define and discuss key terms like "Bleed," "CMYK vs. RGB," "Substrate," "GSM," and "POSM."
  • Material Types: A closer look at different types of vinyl, sunboard, and acrylic, and their specific uses in retail environments.
  • Branding Elements: Understand terms related to in-store branding, such as "Gondola," "End Cap," "Window Display," and "Shop-in-Shop."

End-of-Day Activity

You'll be split into teams for two activities. First, a matching game to connect retail and print terms with visual examples. Second, you'll be given a client brief and will complete a sample estimate worksheet based on it.
