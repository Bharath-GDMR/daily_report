Day 2: Systems & Vendor Familiarization

Objective: To get you comfortable with our internal systems and our external partners. Efficient workflow and strong vendor relationships are key to our success.

Morning Session: Navigating Our Digital Workspace

Efficiency starts with organization. This session will guide you through our digital infrastructure, ensuring you can find what you need, when you need it.

  • Server & Drives Overview: A map of our shared drives, explaining the purpose of each high-level folder.
  • Folder Structures: We'll detail the logic behind our project folder setup, from client folders to asset libraries.
  • File Naming Conventions: Learn our standardized file naming system. This is crucial for version control and easy retrieval of files.

Mid-Morning Session: Our Vendor Network

Our vendors are extensions of our team. This session introduces you to our key partners and the processes for working with them.

  • Vendor Introductions: An overview of our primary vendors for printing, fabrication, and logistics, including their specialties and strengths.
  • Turnaround Times (TAT): Understand the typical timelines for different types of jobs and how to set realistic expectations with clients.
  • Quality Checks: We'll walk you through our quality control checklist, which you'll use to inspect vendor work before it goes to the client.

Afternoon Session: The World of Printing

We'll dive deep into the fundamentals of printing. This isn't just about putting ink on paper; it's a complex industry with a rich history and a dynamic future.

  • Types of Printing: Learn the difference between digital, offset, and screen printing. We'll discuss the pros and cons of each and when to use them.
  • Materials & Substrates: Explore the vast world of materials, from standard paper and cardstock to vinyl, acrylic, and textiles.
  • Applications: See real-world examples of how these techniques and materials come together in everything from business cards to large-scale event branding.

End-of-Day Activity

A practical exercise where you will be given a project brief and asked to navigate the drives to find relevant vendor files and past project examples. You will then present your findings, simulating a real-world task.
