Risk & Mitigation Log

Identified Risks

  • Material Delays: There is a potential risk of delays in the procurement of specialized materials, which could impact the project timeline.
  • On-site Challenges: Unforeseen challenges during on-site installation, such as electrical issues or measurement discrepancies, could cause delays.
  • Vendor Availability: The availability of carpenters and electicians for installation to be closely watched, requiring careful coordination.

Mitigation Plans

  • Early Procurement: To mitigate material delays, all specialized materials will be procured at the earliest possible stage.
  • Contingency Planning: A contingency plan will be in place to address any on-site challenges, including having a backup team of electricians and carpenters on standby.
  • Vendor Coordination: We will work closely with our vendors to ensure timely delivery and installation, with regular follow-ups and a clear communication plan.
